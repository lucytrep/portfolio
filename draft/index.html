Native iOS App · Voice-First Design Tool

Think it. Say it.Draft it.

Draft is a voice-first tool for designers who think out loud. Say the feeling, the vibe, get three directions back instantly. Thought starters, not finished files.

Product Video

What it does

Voice-to-brief in seconds

Speak your idea out loud. Draft turns it into a structured creative brief instantly.

References, pulled automatically

Drop in images or let Draft find them. Every brief comes with visuals that match the mood.

Built for how designers think

Native iOS. Works offline. Fits in your pocket between client calls.

What Draft creates

Palette suggestions pulled from your brief — earthy, electric, muted, or bold. Always tied back to the mood you described.

Interface references that match your direction — typography, layout, and interaction patterns from real apps that feel like what you're building.

Photography, illustration, and texture pulled to match the feeling — not just keywords. Draft finds images that capture the vibe, not just the subject.

Everything pulled together into one shareable board. Color, UI, imagery — formatted and ready to present to a client or kick off a design sprint.
